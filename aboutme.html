Hi! My name is Anton. I am a versatile and accomplished professional ice skater coach, adept at instructing both children and adults in the art of figure skating.

With a wealth of experience, I tailors this coaching methods to suit the specific needs and skill levels of both young aspiring skaters and adults looking to refine their ice skating abilities. I recognize the unique challenges and goals that children and adults bring to the ice, and I skillfully adapts my coaching style to address these differences, ensuring a rewarding experience for each skater.

My coaching philosophy emphasizes a holistic approach, integrating technical precision, artistic expression, and age-appropriate training techniques to cater to the diverse range of his students. Whether working with kids or adults, I always prioritizes creating a positive and supportive learning environment, fostering a sense of enjoyment and accomplishment on the ice.

As a former competitive skater myself, my personally experience enriches my coaching, providing valuable insights and motivation for skaters of all ages.
